Overview
--------

Annolid supports a video-first workflow: open a video, jump to informative frames,
label, track, review corrections, and export.

Use this page as a compatibility entry point for legacy Sphinx docs. For updated
installation and workflow guidance, use the portal pages:

- Installation: https://annolid.com/portal/installation/
- Workflows: https://annolid.com/portal/workflows/
- Tutorials: https://annolid.com/portal/tutorials/

Legacy tutorial videos remain available at:

- https://www.youtube.com/@annolid/videos
